One Body | Many Parts

“The Lord wants to use you for special purposes, so make yourself clean from all evil. Then you will be holy, and the Master can use you. You will be ready for any good work.” ‭
2 Timothy‬ ‭2:21‬ ‭
‬‬ At Great Redemption Ministries, we believe that we are vessels of the Lord as explained in 2 Timothy 2:21. We therefore avail ourselves for His use in building His Kingdom. With respect to this, we pride ourselves with various ministries that help keep our members active in the House of the Lord. Each ministry has a role to play in the overall service, and the leaders of each ministry ensure that their members participate accordingly. In addition to the responsibilities of these ministries in the church, each ministry maintains its own calendar of activities and events, which the members and their leaders draw up, in order to enhance fellowship among themselves. The ministries here at Great Redemption are very receptive of members and are always open to expansion. We therefore encourage all church members to identify themselves with a particular ministry of their interest and actively engage in their various activities.
